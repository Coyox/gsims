ATTENDANCE & PUNCTUALITY - In order to ensure a consistent and stable learning environment for both students and teachers, regular attendance in all registered classes is mandatory. Students should aim to be at least 5 minutes early for their classes. If a student has five (5) or more unexcused absences or lates from any class they will be removed from the class attendance list. If the student wishes to return to the class they will be required to speak with Gobind Sarvar administrators and pay a $50 re-enrollment penalty. If the student misses a total of 10+ classes in a school year or is regularly late, their enrollment will be terminated and they will be required to pay a $150 re-enrollment penalty.

ATTIRE - Gobind Sarvar students are required to wear the following uniforms to classes: Boys: White/blue/orange kurta/chola (top) with white pajama (bottom) and Blue dastar/patka/ramal (head covering) Girls: White/blue/orange kameez/suit (top) with white salwar (bottom) and Blue kaaski/chuni/ramal (head covering) On Youth Days and special events students must wear their uniform and can supplement it with a blue sweater/full sleeve shirt/vest or jacket.
